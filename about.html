Our Story: The Heart of Farmington Toscana Restaurant

Serving the best of traditional Liberian cuisine blended with contemporary Continental dishes since 2017.

The Vision of Farmington Hotel

Farmington Toscana Restaurant was founded on the simple principle that great food brings great joy and peace. Located in the heart of the Robert International Airport, we aim to be more than just a hotel or restaurant—we are a vibrant meeting point for family, friends, and food lovers. Our kitchen is dedicated to preserving the rich, authentic flavors of Liberian cooking while introducing exciting, fresh interpretations of global cuisine.

Our Commitment to Quality and Heritage

We believe that the best meals start with the best ingredients. That’s why we source our ingredients locally and ensure every dish is prepared with passion and attention to detail. From the perfectly spiced Jollof Rice to our fresh seafood, quality is the secret ingredient in everything we serve. Our chefs honor tradition while pushing the boundaries of taste.

A Seamless Digital Experience

Recognizing the modern pace of life, we have developed this comprehensive digital platform. It allows you, our valued customer, to quickly scan a QR code, browse our entire 12-item menu, get **random dish suggestions, and provide **instant feedback, all from the convenience of your mobile device. We embrace technology to enhance your dining experience.
